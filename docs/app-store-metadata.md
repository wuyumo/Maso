# Maso — App Store Metadata

> ⚠️ 1.0 只发 **en-US + 简体中文** 两种语言 (其余 10 种翻译不全已下架, 见 B4)。
> 只用下面 §1 (en-US) 和 §2 (zh-Hans) 两节, 其余语言节是历史存档, 别用。

字段:
- **Subtitle** (30 字符): app 名下面那行
- **Promotional Text** (170 字符): 顶部"What we love"区域,可随时改不需要重新审核
- **Description** (4000 字符): 主描述
- **Keywords** (100 字符): 逗号分隔,用于 search ranking
- **What's New** (4000 字符): 当前版本更新内容(1.0 = intro)

字符限制是 Apple 硬性,超了 App Store Connect 直接拒。

## 1. English (en-US) — Primary

### Name
Maso

### Subtitle (30)
Train. Track. Get stronger.

### Promotional Text (170)
Your personal AI trainer in your pocket. Built for serious lifters who care about progress, not gimmicks. Offline-first, privacy-first, no ads.

### Description (4000)
Maso is a simple, beautiful workout tracker designed for people who actually train. No gamification, no streaks pressure, no ads — just a clean log of every set you do and a clear view of your progress.

**What makes Maso different**

• **Privacy-first.** All your training data lives on your device. No accounts, no email, no analytics.
• **Offline-first.** Works perfectly in basement gyms with no signal.
• **Built for one-handed use.** Big buttons, big numbers, designed for sweaty hands.
• **Three categories.** Strength, cardio, flexibility — one consistent logging model.
• **History as plan.** Your last session is the suggestion for this session.

**Core features**

— **Workout plans.** Drag-to-reorder, multi-set with rest timers, copy from a recommended template or build from scratch.
— **AI workout suggestions (Pro).** Based on your last 14 days, your target muscles, and a database of 873 exercises.
— **Live Activities.** See your rest timer on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. Switch apps mid-workout without losing your place.
— **Apple Health integration.** Workouts sync to your Activity rings and Health timeline.
— **Personal records, quietly.** PRs are detected automatically and shown unobtrusively — no fanfare.
— **Voice-read instructions.** Tap "Listen" on any exercise — Maso reads the simplified instructions aloud in your language.
— **English & 简体中文.** Full UI plus voice-read exercise instructions in both.

**Maso Pro** unlocks:
- AI-generated workout suggestions (uses DeepSeek's API on your behalf)
- Unlimited workout plans
- Full unlimited history
- More themes (coming soon)

Free version is fully functional for everything else. No paywall on basic training logging.

Build something for yourself — train smart, train consistent, see the progress quietly add up.

## App Store Connect 提交清单

每个语言版本提交时:
1. Subtitle — copy 第一行
2. Promotional Text — copy 第二行
3. Description — copy "Description" 块下整段
4. Keywords — copy "Keywords" 一行(逗号分隔)
5. What's New — copy "What's New 1.0.0" 块

注意:
- **不要在 keywords 里塞 brand 名**(Apple 已经把 "Maso" 自动加索引)
- **不要 keyword stuffing** — 避免 "best workout fitness gym free app" 这种,审核会拒
- **Promotional Text 可以随时改不需要重新审核** — 适合放促销 / 活动消息
- **Description 改了要重新审核**

中文版本 (zh-Hans / zh-Hant) 跟 zh 用户量大,适合做主推。日韩用户口味更细,翻译可能需要 native review 再过一遍。
